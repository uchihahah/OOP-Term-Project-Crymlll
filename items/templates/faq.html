Frequently Asked Questions

What is loan interest?

Loan interest is a certain amount of money you need to pay based on your principal amount aside of your basic loan bills

What is saving interest?

Saving interest is a certain amount of money that will be added to your saving account each month based on the ballance of your saving account.

What is overdraft?

Overdraft is an extension of credit that allows you to have your checking account ballance below zero.

How much is the loan interest rate?

The interest rate of the loan account is 0.02% of the principal amount for each month.

How much is the saving interest rate?

The interest rate of the saving account is 0.02% of the current ballance for each month.

How much is the overdraft limit for the checking account?

The overdraft limit for every checking account is IDR 500,000.
